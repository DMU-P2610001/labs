TOM

Tom Bailey is 35, lives in Leicestershire, his hobbies include: Making videos, Gaming, Walks & spending time with his family. Having been in many films before, Tom has picked up on the production side of things & One day he wants to become a documentary film maker. When asked what the best part of making this film Tom answered “just letting myself go, it’s an emotional script & I gave it my all” Tom is nominated for best actor at the biggest award show in England.

CHARLOTTE

Charlotte Jackson is 26 & lives in Leicestershire. She’s a mother to a 2-year-old girl called Violet. She enjoys art & fine wines alongside cooking. This was her first acting role on the big screen & she embraced the whole experience. When asked if she would return for the sequel Charlotte said “who knows, 2020 is a crazy year so far, anything could happen” Charlotte is up for 2 awards: best actress & best newcomer at the awards later this year.
