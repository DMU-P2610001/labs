How I Got Here

I’ve been cooking for as long as I can remember. To me it’s a way of life, but I understand that not everyone can just pick up a knife and a pan to create something off the cuff. That’s why I decided to show you how. Working in many restaurants and kitchens around Leicestershire I have gained much knowledge in the catering industry and I feel it should be shared. Please feel free to look at the recipe page to see how to cook something simple yet tasty and check out my social media in the contact page.
